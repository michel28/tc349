One pensive troubadour, three classically trained bowed-string musicians and one dynamic percussionist come together as Doug Mains & the City Folk.

For BOOKING info contact Doug Mains at doug.mains.music@gmail.com Phone: (517) 899-4655

Doug Mains & the City Folk has played with the following artists: Brendan James, Small Houses, Chris Bathgate, Cotton Jones, Jen Sygit, Sam Corbin, Matt Jones, Pepper Rabbit, Michael Beauchamp, Devin Shelton (of the band Emery), Gifts or Creatures, Theme and Variation, Flatfoot and Brandon John Foote.
